SUMMARY

The purpose of this dashboard
is to give the end user a simple
and straight forward dashboard
to see how email product is per-
forming and what effect it has on
their total revenue and conversion.

INTRODUCTION

This dashboard shows the importance of email product and its performance on campaigns over time. Also, will help in measuring engagement growth over time to avoid missing out on valuable insights and any significant changes alerts that marketing team should take an action towards. Data is visualized according to this diagram. Not to mention that, the most important data is placed at top left corner of the dashboard.

PROJECT GOALS

  • To give the customers an easy and simple view of how their Shop Email Product is performing.
  • Provide important operational information and key performance indicators for the marketing team on cost per acquisition.
  • Clean layout with simple visuals (graphs and charts) not busy colors to avoid making the user distracted from the data.
  • Get an overall felling very quickly.
  • Dive deep in details when needed

Define User Personas

    Conducted phone interviews with two personas. Each interview took approximately 30-35 mins this helped to understand users' needs, experiences, behaviours and goals. It also helped to identify with the target user designing for.
