PROBLEM STATEMENT

Buyers want to often buy products that are only available in other countries ,however, sometimes it is not possible for them to travel and get what they desire.

Ovni is trying to develop an easy-to-use platform and App that connect travelers with buyers. Buyer make a wish and add all details of the product they would like to buy so that traveler who are going to the destination where the product can be found,can contact them and get the product for a delivery fee.

PROJECT GOALS

  • Developing MVP to serve as a proof of concept
  • Acquire as much buyers and travelers as possible
  • Devleoping an easy to use interface, given that the concept is brand new in Egypt
  • Website to be designed friendly as the app is on its way to be published in the app store
  • Introduce mobile and tablet optimisation
  • After finishing the proof of concept, gathering as much as possible information from both travelers and buyers to highlight the pain points to avoid it while designing mobile app

Define User Personas

GATHERING INSIGHTS

    After collecting the recordings from the user interviews, I conducted affinity mapping with my teammate to synthesise the pains identified. We grouped these problems under common themes and features in the platforms.
